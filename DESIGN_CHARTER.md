## 1. Visual Language System

### Color Palette: "Event Horizon"

* **Void:** `#030305` (Deep warm charcoal/black background).
* **Starlight:** `#E2E8F0` (Primary text/Silver).
* **Event Horizon:** `#6366F1` (Indigo - Brands & "Classical" elements).
* **Active Signal:** `#00F0FF` (Cyan - Cursors, active states, "Jazz" spikes).
* **Alert:** `#FF9F1C` (Orange - Critical data points).

### Typography

* **Display:** `Cabinet Grotesk` (Editorial, variable weight).
* **Technical:** `JetBrains Mono` (Code, metadata, navigation).

### Interactions

* **Gravity Cursor:** Magnetic pull on buttons.
* **Lenis Scroll:** Heavy, smooth inertia scrolling.
* **Scramble Text:** Headers decode themselves (`X#9F` -> `EXPERIENCE`).
